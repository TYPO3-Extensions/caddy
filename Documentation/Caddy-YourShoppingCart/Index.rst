Caddy - your Shopping Cart
==========================

Caddy is your shopping cart. See http://typo3-caddy.de. Manual at
http://typo3-caddy.de/typo3conf/ext/caddy/doc/manual.pdf. You can use
caddy for your own needs. You need a database with products or any
other items and the extension powermail. You can install Caddy with
one mouse click. Quick Shop is using Caddy.
